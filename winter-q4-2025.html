Takeaways for the Rock

  • Snow Removal was the winter workhorse. The "It's Impossible [to hire]" Snow Removal video drove the most winter leads and customers, and had the best hook rate (29.7%) — strongest scroll-stopper of the season.
  • Winning seasonal angle = winter-as-hiring-window urgency. "Winter is the best time to build your crew" (VHK55) generated cheap leads and converted; pair it with the Snow Removal vertical.
  • Hook is strong, hold is the leak. Winter video stops the scroll (28–30% hook) but retention drops fast (10–17% hold). Fixing the mid-video is the biggest lever — borrow the retention structure from the "3 Reasons" body.
  • Dear Santa hooked but didn't convert. Novelty stop, no downstream intent (0 customers). Fun creative, weak funnel — deprioritize.
  • Never scaled The big "Winter CR3" batch (50+ variations) across Snow Removal / HVAC / Plumbing / Chimney / Electrical — plus the New Year set — ran at ~$0 spend. Untested inventory, not proven losers.
  • No standalone winter copy scaled. Every winter copy hook (BeatTheWinter, WinterIsTheBest, WinterExposesLastDecember) got test-level spend and 0 leads. Winning winter messaging lived in the video scripts.
